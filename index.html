    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

    What are research peptides?

    Research peptides are synthetic amino acid chains used exclusively for scientific and laboratory research. All products are research-grade compounds intended for laboratory use only. Not for human consumption.

    How do I place an order?

    Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and complete payment using cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, SOL, or USDT). Your order will be confirmed automatically once payment is detected on the blockchain.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and USDT. Payments are processed directly with zero fees through our self-hosted payment system. Browser wallets like MetaMask and Phantom are supported for one-click payment.

    How long does shipping take?

    Orders are typically processed within 2-5 business days. Domestic (US) shipping takes 3-7 business days after processing. You will receive a tracking number once your order ships.

    Do you ship internationally?

    Currently, we primarily serve customers within the United States. International shipping may be available for select kit orders. Contact us for details.

    What is your purity guarantee?

    All products undergo rigorous HPLC testing with purity levels of 99% or higher. Each product includes a Certificate of Analysis (COA) with lot-specific purity data.

    Can I get a refund?

    Due to the nature of research compounds, we cannot accept returns on opened products. If you receive a damaged or incorrect order, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery for a resolution.

    How do I store my peptides?

    Lyophilized (freeze-dried) peptides should be stored at -20°C in a desiccated environment. Once reconstituted, store at 2-8°C and use within the recommended timeframe.

    Do you offer discount codes?

    Yes! Join our mailing list for exclusive deals, or check our Discord community for active promo codes. Affiliate codes are also available for content creators.

    Is my payment secure?

    Absolutely. Cryptocurrency payments are processed directly on the blockchain with no intermediaries. For wallet payments, transactions are signed locally in your browser wallet — we never have access to your private keys.
